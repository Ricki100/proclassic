Our Services

Radial & Cross Ply Tyres is in the business of supplying, delivering new tyres and tubes and repairing of old tyres at a guarantee. Through a collaborative partner and engineering personnel, the objectives of Radial & Cross Ply Tyres are:

  • To operate at the highest possible safety standards.
  • To achieve maximum tyre and machine efficiencies at minimum operating cost to the company.
  • To extract maximum tyre value through focus on the continuous improvement of tyre related elements.
  • To reduce tyre operating costs including inflationary escalations.
  • To forecast on tyre purchasing
  • To have technical data on the status of your tyres
