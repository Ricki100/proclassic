About Us

Radial & Cross Ply Tyres is the trade name for Proclassic Trading (Private) Limited which is a wholly owned Zimbabwean limited company duly incorporated under the Companies Act (Chapter 24:03). It was registered on the 25th of July, 2003, with registration number 11399/2003. The company started only as a tyre repair and re-treading till 2013, when it also became an official tyre distributor for Sportrak, Diamondback, Triangle and Superway tyres.

Principal Activity

The principal activities of Proclassic are the sourcing and distribution of the full range of automobile tyres, tubes and accessories as well as repairing, retreading, fitting, balancing and alignment thereof. It also provides fleet and tyre management services and technical training for corporate with large fleets of vehicles.

Vision

To build a solid investment and trading enterprise on the basis of service excellence to its customers within Zimbabwe, the Southern African, Africa and beyond.

Mission

Proclassic strives to provide a wide range of renowned brands of tyres, tubes and other automobile accessories to private, public and non-governmental organizations in Zimbabwe, the Southern African, Africa and beyond.
